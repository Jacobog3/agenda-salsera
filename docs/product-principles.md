# Exploraguate Product Reference

## Product Statement

Exploraguate is a mobile-first agenda for salsa and bachata events in Guatemala.

It takes information that is currently scattered across WhatsApp chats, flyers, and posts and turns it into a clear discovery experience.

## Core Value

Users should be able to answer these questions in seconds:

- What events are happening this week?
- Where are they?
- How much do they cost?
- Who is organizing them?
- How do I contact them?

## Design Principles

1. Utility before narrative.
2. Mobile scanning before desktop composition.
3. Real scene signals before generic marketing copy.
4. Flyers are source material, not decoration.
5. Guatemala should feel present through places, venues, and community context, not tourism clichés.

## What The Home Page Must Do

- Show what the product is immediately.
- Surface upcoming events fast.
- Reflect the visual language of the local dance scene.
- Feel modern, calm, and trustworthy.
- Avoid filler sections or abstract messaging.
